Reception Hall

At each of our Temple Inn & Suites location, we have a reception hall that is available for rental. We team with local businesses to provide accomodations such as tables, chairs, lights, decor, etc, as well as catering and food service options. It is a beautiful space that has been utilized for wedding receptions, luncheons, family history conferences, and many more events that benefit by being near a temple. You can see our gallery photos from past events.
