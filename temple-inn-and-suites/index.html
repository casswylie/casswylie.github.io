What We Do

Our hotels were opened because there was a need not being met. We all want to serve the Lord and do His holy work in the temple, whether it be for ourselves or others, through missionary work, etc. Now there are places to stay or change for when you travel far to the temple, a reception hall for other services, a shuttle to and from the temple, short-term babysitting for those with young children, and many other things to help make your experience at the temple even more enjoyable and worry free.
