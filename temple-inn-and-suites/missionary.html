Full-time Missionaries

One of the big things we are here to accomodate are our full-time missionaries. We provide long-term stay accomodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple and visitors center missionaries.

What We Have to Offer

We offer extended stay suites with 1 king bed for those who serve as a married couple as well as suites with 2 bedrooms and 2 queen beds for two couples and penthouse suites with 2 bedrooms and 4 twin beds to accomodate two companionship missionaries.
