Contrast

Amazon

How Its Used

Amazon uses contrast in its web design very well in my opinion. For starters, the colors on this main page work well together and the dark contrast from the heading to the content of retail items shows a differentiation between information vs. sale items.

Another place there is contast on this site it the text. The text color in the header contrasts drastically with the dark blue background and the white words. It makes it easy to read and appealing to the eye. Throughout the site, the text size also contrasts from each other in areas where you will know what is a title, description, link, etc, because they differ in size.
