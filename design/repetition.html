Repetition

Brigham Young University - Idaho

How Repetition is Used

The Brigham Young University - Idaho website does a great job at demonstrating the principle of repetition. The main blue color used on the header is also found at the "Live Chat" link, as well as the links for news and campus information, and other important listed information such as events. In the header, there is also the white typography that is repeated throughout the web page on the heading image, the live chat link and later on in the footer and other areas as well.

Overall, the web page keeps well to the repetition of the colors used.
