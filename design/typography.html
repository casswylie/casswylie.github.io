Typography

Family Search

How it is Used

Family Search is a site that I have always admired. When looking at its typography, they are very consistant and they use two fonts that contrast but work very well together. They use a classic bold serif font for all headings and a sans-serif for all other text. It is easily differentiated between titles and content because of these slight differences. This kind of design makes a site look more professional and less amatuer.
