Proximity

Discover

How it's Used

On the Discover website, I feel that the proximity in the design really helps to know where you are and where you can go. The spacing between headings and clickable links is close enough to know which link you are to click, and each different button are spaced further apart to show that they are different. Margins and padding can take design a long ways through creating a sense of proximity and I think Discover did a great job at that on their site.
