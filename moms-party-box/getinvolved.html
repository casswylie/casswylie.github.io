How Can You Get Involved?

  1. Share our message on Social Media to help us reach more people.
  2. Come to one of our service events.
  3. Make a birthday card(s) to uplift and encourage a child on their birthday and mail them to us to put in the boxes:
    Mom's Party box
    10939 N Alpine Hwy Suite 121
    Highland, UT 84003
    United States
  4. Sponser a Mom's Party Box by donating financially or by gathering needed supplies:
    Birthday candles, table runners, gift bags, cake mix, frosting, cupcake liners, napkins, balloons, and a birthday card.See local businesses here who have sponsered birthday boxes.

Join us at our next service event and help celebrate childhood and help kids feel special on their day!
