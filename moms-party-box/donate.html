Donate

Every donation goes to help a child feel loved and celebrated on their bithday! Inside each Party Box you'll find supplies like cake mix, frosting, plates, cups, candles, banners, birthday cards, an age appropriate present, and more. We provide boxes for young children up through teenagers because there is no age limit to feeling special!

As a 501(c)(3) organization all donations are tax deductible. And right now, if you donate at least $10 you'll receive one of our super cute Happy Birthday banners as a gift! Just include your address in the "Notes" section of your donation and we'll send you some happy mail.
